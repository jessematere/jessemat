OVERVIEW ON PHOTOGRAPHY

Photography is a diverse area that one would like to venture. The avenues includes photo-journalism, studio photography, landscape photography or even bieng a paparazzi. Some people learn the art of photography from others while some will go to school to aquire the general knowledge. There is this cliche that whenever I take a camera it guide me on what is required; "greate photos are made not taken". This is actually true because your judgement on the composition in your frame is what will tell the type of results you will achieve. For the basic knowledge, the guiding principle are three features that will determine. they include;ISO, aparture, and the shutterspeed . These are the ones that will tell the nature of your work and the type of a photo you will have since different photos will tell different story depending on your composition. With the advancement of technology we have seen mirrorless cameras replacing the modern DSLR that are current in the market. Also the issue of focusing, campanies have advanced where by the settings are made simple like focusing point and also eye ditactor for enhancing focusing technique. This modernization are revolutionalizing photography where these techniques are being simplified.

Lastly, majority of my photos above have the bokeh effect and are either close up or medium shots. That is my nature when it comes to portrait shots because the shallow depth field of view communicates your skills in managing both the foreground and the background in my frame composition. This isn't the case when it come to landsacpe photography because with the kind of shot nature always has some additional story to tell about the main subject. For example a shot of a lion in the park, it should have a wide depth field of view because the sorroundings tells additional information about the main subject, like if it is dry or shrubs like environment, it means the drought is hitting hard, this should raise concern.
